Software on a budget.

The top 4 software consulting firms rake in hundreds of billions of dollars from customers in a market expected to reach $588.14 billion by 2030 (Mordor Intelligence). Unfortunately, those rates do not always coincide with positive outcomes. We still see bloated budgets, missed timelines, and meandering scope.

We take a different approach, not motivated by profit margins that reward individuals who are often the least informed about technology.

It involves being transparent.

Instead of building software that works for us, we explore what's feasible for your needs and investment. This could mean:

  • Implementing no- or low-code tools like Knack, Zapier, Make, Shopify, or WordPress.
  • Leveraging free and open source technologies like WordPress, Keycloak, ActivePieces, or Medusa.
  • Embedding into your software development team to help out with architecture, modeling, design, and specifications.
  • Creating custom software in a manner that ignores the buzz words and embraces iterative feedback cycles.

Our Vision

Transparency and affordability in technology.

  • We show you the direct rate we pay our partner freelancers, with any corresponding markup we employ to keep the internet on.
  • We do not take referral bonuses from vendors. We recommend products and services based on the quality and solution fit for those products and services in your ecosystem to avoid bias.
  • We embrace iterative feedback loops with end users (or the closest proxies of end users) and do not leave you out of the loop.
  • We believe in the practice of software engineering and its value to organizations; we do not believe in outsourcing exclusively for cheap labor, as this has long been a practice that's haunted many industries and leads to not only the exploitation of labor but poor outcomes in product quality.
