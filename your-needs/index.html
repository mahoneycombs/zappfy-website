Our Vision

Transparency and affordability in technology.

  • We show you the direct rate we pay our partner freelancers, with any corresponding markup we employ to keep the internet on.
  • We do not take referral bonuses from vendors. We recommend products and services based on the quality and solution fit for those products and services in your ecosystem to avoid bias.
  • We embrace iterative feedback loops with end users (or the closest proxies of end users) and do not leave you out of the loop.
  • We believe in the practice of software engineering and its value to organizations; we do not believe in outsourcing exclusively for cheap labor, as this has long been a practice that’s haunted many industries and leads to not only the exploitation of labor but poor outcomes in product quality.
